Motivation Lab: Video Experiment

Welcome to the video experiment by the Motivated Memory Lab. If you would like to begin the experiment, please press the start button below.

In this experiment you will be asked to watch several videos. Each video will begin with an empty canvas and will slowly reveal itself through one continuous line stroke. Throughout the video, try to figure out what is being drawn. Record your answers in the space provided.end with a drawing of some sort. At the end of the video, you will be asked to write down what the final image is, and rate how interested you were in the video and how fast you thought the line was moving.

When watching these videos please try your best to focus your attention on the drawing to give us the best understanding of how and when you can identify what it being drawn.

Try to use the most common English words for drawing depictions. For example, if you see an image of a hamster write "hamster" instead of "animal" or "Winter White Dwarf hamster".

Please also do not try to hit the Refresh button during the experiment.
