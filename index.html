Adcock Lab: Video Experiment

Welcome to the video experiment by the Adcock Lab. If you would like to begin the experiment, please press the start button below.

In this experiment you will be asked to watch several videos. The videos will begin with an empty canvas and will end with a drawing of some sort. Midway through the video, at any point, we ask that you pause the video and write down your prediction as to what the image will be. At the end of the video, you will be asked to write down what the final image is, and if you were correct.

When writing down our predictions, please give broad, general names for what is depicted. For instance, if you see a “puppy” or a “baby Chihuahua”, please just write down “Dog”.

Please also do not try to hit the Refresh button during the experiment.
